Simple Rules

What to do when the day goes sideways

If tired Shorten the day

Eat nearby and use Uber, FREENOW, or an official black cab back to the hotel.

If it rains Move indoors

Shift toward markets, cafes, covered areas, and indoor stops without guilt.

If separated Stop and reconnect

Stop moving, call each other, share location, and meet at the last agreed landmark or hotel.

If lost Use the hotel as home base

Open the hotel map, ask staff inside a station/shop/hotel, and head back to Victoria if unsure.
