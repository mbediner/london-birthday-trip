When Things Change

Simple fallback rules

If tired

Shorten the walk, eat nearby, and use Uber or a black cab back to the hotel.

If it rains

Shift toward markets, cafes, shops, covered areas, and indoor stops. Keep the day easy.

If separated

Stop moving, call each other, share location, and meet at the last agreed major landmark.

If lost

Open the hotel map, use TfL Go, or ask staff inside a shop, station, hotel, or restaurant.
